About Us

WHO ARE WE?

Established in 1987 as HARSIDDH INDUSTRIES, our company has proudly completed 35 years in the industry. Now operating as HARSIDDH PHARMAMACH PVT LTD, we have evolved over the years, driven by our commitment to excellence and innovation. With a rich heritage and extensive experience in the field, we specialize in manufacturing complete aseptic packaging line solutions for liquid and injectable products in sterile conditions, utilizing the latest advanced technologies and innovative ideas.

Our Experties

Backed by our long and vast experience in the industry, we have honed our expertise to deliver satisfactory performance round-the-clock. Our machinery solutions are designed to meet the highest standards of quality and reliability, offering flexibility to adapt to the specific needs of each customer. Whether you require individual machines or a complete production line, we provide solutions tailored to your requirements.

Innovation and Development

At HARSIDDH, we are committed to continuous innovation and development, ensuring that our solutions remain at the forefront of the industry. We strive to incorporate the latest advancements in technology and design into our machinery, offering our customers cutting-edge solutions that meet their evolving needs.

  • Customized solutions tailored to client needs
  • Strict adherence to quality standards
  • Excellent after-sales support and maintenance services

Global Reach

With a client list that includes renowned companies in India and abroad, we have established ourselves as one of the rapidly growing exporters of pharmaceutical equipment. Our sales team, project management, and after-sales service operate at a global level, working tirelessly to create HARSIDDH's brand value in the global market.

  • Renowned exporter of pharmaceutical equipment.
