Chronicle of Harsiddh Pharma's Journey

Start

1987

With a vision to provide pharmaceutical companies with best state of the art machinery a small manufacturing facitliy was established at Rakhiyal Ahmedabad by Late Shri Popatbhai M. Mistry

1988

India’s first Slant
Single Head Ampoule
filling & sealing
machine is developed

1991

India’s first Vertical Single Head Ampoule Filling and Sealing Machine

2001

The company shifted to a
bigger state-of-the-art
ultra-modern manufacturing facility
of 40,000 sq feet at VATVA Ahmedabad.

2005

Rotary Vial Washing Machine was developed.

2007

Entrusted with the manufacturing capacity and quality of HARSIDDH format part division for imported lines.

2008

Vertical and Horizontal Cartonator Machine was developed.

2013

Rotary Ampoule Washing Machine was developed.

2016

Hi-Speed Ampoule & Vial Line was developed

2017

Nasal Line and Ophthalmic Filling Line were developed.

 

2018

Veterinary Plastic PFS Machine was developed.

2021

MDI Line was developed.
